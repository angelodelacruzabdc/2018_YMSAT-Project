S T A T E O F S C I E N C E
Innovation in our country exists, but not as much as we would like.
S T A T E O F S C I E N C E
As of last year, the Philippines has ranked 74th among 128 economies in terms of overall scientific advancement and innovation, as per the Global Innovation Index Report

The Philippines garnered a dismal score of 31.8 out of 100 points
S T A T E O F S C I E N C E
Technology used in our infrastructure and industries are considered subpar to a lot of our foreign neighbors

 Some of our current technologies are just hand-me-downs from foreign nations such as the US and China which shows that we cannot really manufacture our own scientific/technological instruments for progress.
S T A T E O F S C I E N C E
Budget for the Department of Science and Technology is not prioritized by the current administration which may inhibit the rate at which research or scientific advancements are made.

 In the proposed budget of the DOST for 2019, there has been an estimated P1.4 billion budget cut from the current 2018 budget of the department.
S T A T E O F S C I E N C E
There are not a lot of Filipinos who would want to engage in a career in Science and Technology.

Most of our scientists in the country receive wages that are not considered “high” by societal standards.
A job as a scientist isn’t very appealing to most of Filipinos who yearn to get a high salary from gold-collar jobs.
